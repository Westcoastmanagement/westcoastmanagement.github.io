Our Mission

At West Coast Management Ltd, we are dedicated to providing exceptional contracting and management services to kiwifruit orchards of all sizes. Our mission is to help our clients achieve their goals by maximizing productivity, promoting sustainability, and enhancing the overall efficiency of their operations.

To achieve this, we leverage our expertise and experience in the kiwifruit industry, working closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and challenges. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering high-quality services, from planting and pruning to irrigation, pest management, harvesting, and marketing.

We also believe in promoting sustainable practices that minimize environmental impact and support long-term success. Whether our clients need assistance with day-to-day operations or require long-term planning and strategic guidance, we are here to help every step of the way.

Ultimately, our goal is to become the go-to partner for kiwifruit orchards in need of reliable, effective, and efficient contracting and management services. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients, based on mutual trust, respect, and a shared commitment to success.
